Insights 							
							
Basic Insights							
							
The dataset contains a diverse group of investors with varying preferences.							
A majority of investors actively monitor their investments.							
Mutual Funds and Equity are among the most preferred investment avenues.							
Gold and Fixed Deposits are chosen as safer investment options.							
							
							
							
Gender-Based Insights							
							
Male investors show higher inclination towards Equity and Mutual Funds.							
Female investors prefer relatively safer options like FD and Gold.							
Overall participation is balanced across genders.							
							
							
							
Age-Based Insights							
							
Younger investors (<30) prefer high-risk investments like Equity.							
Middle-aged investors (30–40) prefer Mutual Funds (balanced approach).							
Older investors prefer low-risk options like FD, Bonds, and PPF.							
							
							
							
Investment Preference Insights							
							
Equity provides the highest expected returns among all avenues.							
Fixed Deposits and Bonds provide stable but lower returns.							
Investors diversify their portfolio across multiple options.							
							
							
							
Objective vs Investment Insights							
							
Investors with wealth creation goals prefer Equity and Mutual Funds.							
Investors focused on safety prefer FD, PPF, and Bonds.							
There is a clear alignment between objectives and investment choices.							
							
							
							
Risk Analysis Insights							
							
Most investors fall under high-risk category due to higher allocation in Equity/Mutual Funds.							
A segment of investors still prefers low-risk instruments for stability.							
							
							
							
Duration Insights							
							
Short-term investors prefer FD and Gold.							
Long-term investors prefer Equity and Mutual Funds.							
							
							
							
Behavioural Insights							
							
Returns and safety are the major factors influencing investment decisions.							
Investors consider both risk and return before investing.							
							
							
							
Recommendation Insight							
							
High-risk investors are best suited for Equity and Mutual Funds.							
Low-risk investors should prefer FD, Bonds, and PPF.							
							
							
							
Summary:							
							
Investors show a mix of risk-taking and safety preferences.							
Equity and Mutual Funds dominate for growth, while FD and Bonds ensure stability.							
Age, objective, and risk appetite strongly influence investment decisions.
